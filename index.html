college admission service

An organization dedicated to supporting international students in their college applications journey.

We help with college applications, essay writing, resume creation, tutoring services for SAT, ACT, and TOEFL etc. This service is dedicated for students who are applying to universities in the US, UK, EU, and Canada.

Services

  • Essay Writing:


    One of the core parts of a college application is your essay or personal statement. It is the only way that the recruiters have to get to know you as a person. So writing a strong, personable, and unique essay should be every student’s goal, and I am hoping to help you with that essay-writing process

  • Resume Building:


    As you transition into higher studies, it is important to start building a resume that highlights your academic achievements in a professional manner. A resume should be short but impactful, and with some guidance you can build a polished resume for career opportunities.

  • Tutoring:


    SAT, ACT, and TOEFL are some of the main exams that I tutor for.

  • College Admission Package:


    This includes everything that the college admission process entails. It will be tailored to the student’s needs as they apply for college, and will include all the services mentioned above. I will help you from start to finish in the college admission process if you choose this option.

Process

  1. One on one zoom meeting with the student to assess their college admission needs.
  2. 1 hour of free consultation
  3. Student can decide to opt for our services after the demo if they feel we are a good fit for their needs.

Prices

  1. 30 USD per hour for any service (including esay writing, resume editing, tutoring).
  2. 200 USD for College Admission Package
